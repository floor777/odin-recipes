protein shake

Description

This dish is a mix of peanut butter, milk, protein powder, honey, and oatmeal that adds up to enough calories for any weightlifters nutritional needs

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • milk
  • protein powder
  • honey
  • oatmeal

Steps

  1. Pour one cup of milk into the blender
  2. Put 2 scoops of protein powder into the blender
  3. Put 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  4. Put 1 tablespoon of honey
  5. Put 1/2 a cup of oatmeal
